Custom Programs

Enhance the management and leadership capabilities of your organization to sustain and grow your business performance. OSB Executive Education partners with you to design programs that specifically meet your organization's development goals. Our team of experts develop unique learning experiences specifically tailored for the skill sets of your leaders and executives. Our programs can be delivered at your preferred location or on campus according to your convenience.

Customization Process: Our Approach

Our programs development process begins with the completion of a Preliminary Needs Assessment that helps us learn more about your organization, your objectives, the audience, and the context of your needs. We will then analyze your needs to design, develop, and implement an academically fit and highly tailored program addressing the target objectives.
