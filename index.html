Open Enrollment Programs

PRICING ANALYTICS

This program focuses on pricing as a competitive driver balancing the trade between faster resource/inventory turnover and higher margins through data analytics capabilities. While advances in analytics are rapidly transforming pricing practices, this program sheds light on the enduring principles that form the foundation of effective pricing strategy.

Face-to-Face December 2-3, 2026 2 Days AUB Academic and Executive Hub in Dubai
Learn More
DESIGN THINKING

This Design Thinking program explores the needed requirements for enabling customer focused innovation that is key for facing increasingly competitive environments. It aims to support aspiring entrepreneurs and professionals exploring intra-organizational innovation practices. The program aims to develop the ability to frame stakeholders’ needs and generate innovative and feasible solutions to address these needs.

Face-to-Face July 28-29-30, 2026 3 Days AUB - OSB in Lebanon
Learn More
AI IN SUPPLY CHAIN

This program focuses on real-world AI applications in production, demand forecasting, inventory management, logistics optimization, and risk mitigation. Participants will learn how AI-driven systems shape modern supply chains and how to interpret their outputs to make informed strategic decisions.

Face-to-Face September 1-2-3, 2026 3 Days AUB - OSB in Lebanon
Learn More
FAMILY BUSINESS GOVERNANCE

This unique program equips family business leaders with tools and frameworks needed to design governance systems that manage complexity and ensure continuity without compromising family harmony. Topics include family governance structures, board composition, preserving family's wealth, planning succession, and managing conflicts.

Face-to-Face October 13-14-15, 2026 3 Days AUB - OSB in Lebanon
