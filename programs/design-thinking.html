The Program

With today's rapidly evolving customer needs, organizations are under increasing pressure to innovate and constantly adapt to their customers' changing requirements. The shift from a centralized innovation team to a team-based, cross-functional effort requires fundamental changes to the traditional innovation mindset and the methodologies teams adopt.

The Innovate to Elevate Program, developed by the American University of Beirut's Suliman S. Olayan School of Business (OSB), explores the requirements for enabling customer-focused innovation—a key strategy for facing increasingly competitive environments. It focuses on empowering teams to turn innovative ideas into execution through design thinking.
