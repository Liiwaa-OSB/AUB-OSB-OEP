Overview

CEDAR Program, developed by the American University of Beirut, Suliman S. Olayan School of Business (OSB), is a learning experience designed specifically for C-suite leaders and senior executives. This program bridges the gap between disruptive technological forces and the timeless demands of strategic clarity and cognitive precision. Over the course of this program, participants will master three interconnected pillars crucial to modern enterprise survival: The 5th Industrial Revolution Leader, Strategy: Resilience & Value Creation, and The Architecture of Choice.

The AI revolution has fundamentally redrawn the boundaries of executive responsibility. It is no longer enough to lead digital transformation; senior leaders must now actively harmonize unprecedented AI capabilities with human-centric purpose and systemic organizational resilience with deep ethical responsibility.

The Three Core Pillars

Our curriculum is structured into three distinct yet reinforcing learning blocks, carefully curated to build a multidimensional leadership identity:

Pillar 1: The 5th Industrial Revolution Leader

Technology + Humanity
Lead an AI-centered culture grounded in ethics and core values. Synthesize digital trends to prepare for a future where technology and humanity coexist harmoniously.

Pillar 2: Strategy: Resilience & Value Creation

Value + Resilience
Deconstruct the process of identifying market opportunities and challenges, crafting compelling value propositions, and mitigating high-stakes risks.

Pillar 3: The Architecture of Choice

Cognitive Precision + Influence
Dive into the psychology of cognitive biases that distort professional judgment. You will master the art of ethical influence: steering organizational outcomes through empathy and evidence-based precision.

How You Benefit

Geographic Agility

Capitalize on the cross-border insights gained from working across major executive education hubs.

De‑Biased Decision Making

Walk away with concrete frameworks to optimize your organization's internal choice architecture.

Lasting Professional Capital

Connect deeply with an elite cohort of cross‑functional C‑suite peers navigating identical corporate complexities.

Program Details

2 days · on Campus

The 5th Industrial Revolution Leader

Technology + Humanity
This session introduces topics and tools that are central to purposeful and digital leadership. Participants will explore frameworks around ethical issues faced by managers and organizations as well as the latest findings on human behavior to inform ethical decisions and digital transformation strategies specifically involving AI.

2 days · on Campus

Strategy: Resilience & Value Creation

Value + Resilience
The module focuses on the fundamental understanding of strategy formulation and implementation. Participants will be introduced to what constitutes a strategy choice of how and where to compete to create and/or leverage value. Strategy is composed of differentiators that constitute competitive advantage.

2 days · on Campus

The Architecture
of Choice

Cognitive Precision + Influence
This session will explore the psychological and social forces that derail our rational thinking and how often (and why) do we turn a blind eye to objective information. Participants will learn to manage our biases and those of others in decision-making.

Program Academic Overseer

Yusuf Sidani, PhD

Professor of Leadership and Business Ethics
Dean, Suliman S. Olayan School of Business – American University of Beirut

Dr. Yusuf Sidani is Dean of the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business at the American University of Beirut. His work combines strong academic rigor with a practical focus on executive education and leadership development. He has an established research profile in leadership, ethics, corporate social responsibility, and management in emerging markets. His scholarship has contributed to advancing responsible leadership and organizational effectiveness, particularly in the Middle East. Beyond academia, Dr. Sidani has extensive experience designing and delivering executive programs for senior leaders across sectors, including healthcare, public institutions, and multinational organizations. His expertise spans leadership development, decision-making, change management, and executive effectiveness. He is also an active consultant and advisor, supporting organizations in strengthening leadership capacity, improving governance, and driving strategic transformation. Dr. Sidani is widely recognized for bridging theory and practice and for his commitment to developing responsible leaders who create sustainable impact.
