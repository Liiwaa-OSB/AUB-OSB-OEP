The Program

Family businesses are built on legacy, values, and relationships—but sustaining them across generations requires governance that is both strategic and deeply human.

Family Business Governance Program, developed by the American University of Beirut, Suliman S. Olayan School of Business (OSB), blends academic rigor with real-world relevance, helping participants navigate the unique dynamics that make family enterprises both powerful and fragile. This immersive three-day program equips family business leaders with the tools and frameworks needed to design governance systems that manage complexity and ensure continuity without compromising family harmony.

Why This Program

Innovative

Updated governance for family businesses

It delivers an updated perspective on governance tailored specifically to family-owned businesses.

Practical

Hands‑on tools & techniques

It provides you with hands-on experience of the application of tools and techniques.

Short and Effective

Fits your busy schedule

It is designed to suit your busy schedule and aims to provide you with the know-how in 3 days.

Highly Engaging

Active, collaborative learning

It includes case studies, interactive discussions, hands-on exercises, and individual contributions.

Program Instructor

GEORGES SAMARA, PhD

Associate Professor of Entrepreneurship and Family Business
Olayan School of Business – American University of Beirut

Georges Samara, Ph.D., is an Associate Professor of Entrepreneurship and Family Business at the Olayan School of Business, American University of Beirut. He is the winner of the extraordinary doctorate award, along with more than 10 international awards for his published research. His research has focused on family business, entrepreneurship, and corporate social responsibility and sustainability, and he has published more than 50 academic articles, editorials, books and book chapters. For more than eight years, he consults family businesses in the Gulf, where he has developed extensive practice in advising, coaching, and guiding family enterprises through complex transitions. His expertise extends to preparing comprehensive reports and expert opinions for judicial bodies, offering courts nuanced insights into family business governance, succession disputes, and sustainability strategies. Dr. Samara brings a unique blend of academic rigor and practical engagement.

AUB alumni, looking to reconnect with the AUB network and renew their learning journey.
